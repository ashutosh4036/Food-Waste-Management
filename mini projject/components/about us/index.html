About us

This website will definitely bring a great change in the society by motivating them towards social work and also by making NGO's easy to find the donor People will get to know the sources where they can manage the extra food or where they can donate because sometimes people wants to do some social work but do not know exactly how to do that and where to do that And sometimes people want to do donation but do not have the time for that so in that also our website will help the people to donate at right place with innovative manner and on trusted platform.
People will now also know the value of food when they see the amount of waste food people daily register on our website. Also people will get the blessing from the poor for their help.

Motivation

Each year, 15 million children die of hunger-related causes. This means that, every day, throughout the world, 40 000 children die.While FAO estimated food waste to be 214 kilo calories per day (Kcal/day) per capita in 2015, the new model estimated food waste as 527 Kcal/day per capita for the same year.
Consumers around the world may be wasting more than twice as much food as commonly believed, according to a study.So we thought we can help people so they can get to eat everyday.

Every grain matters

Cutting food waste is a delicious way of saving money, helping to feed the world and protect the planet
